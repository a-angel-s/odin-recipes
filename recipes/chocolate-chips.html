how to make chocolate chip cookies

yummy and warm cookies, perfect for any day! :D

ingredients

  • 1 cup of soft butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp hot water
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. mix butter, white and brown sugar until smooth
  3. add eggs one at a time and then add vanilla
  4. dissolve baking soda into hot water and add to mixture with salt
  5. finally, add flour and chocolate chips
  6. scoop out balls of batter and place on greased pan
  7. bake for ten minutes or until browned
